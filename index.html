Independent Research Initiative • Founder & Principal Researcher • June 2025 – Present • St. Kitts & Nevis

encodedemotion.org
  • Independently conceptualized, designed, and executed mixed-methods studies (N≈133+) investigating how emotional reasoning, heuristics, metacognition, and cognitive bias drive psychological resistance to Artificial Intelligence.
